Build an agentic AI application to showcase the components of agentic AI like action, plan, think, memory etc.

The overall use case is as follows:

An editorial office gets hundreds of headlines everyday from around the world. 
These headlines can belong to any news category - from politics, sports, technology etc.
Not every headline is attractive enough to be considered for publication.

The headlines that are selected are then sent to the respective editors who will then generate content.

With this overview, the following points are to be considered:

1) Analyse one headline at a time from a list of news headlines
2) Classify it
3) Give a Relevancy score between 1-5. This score reflects how relevant the headline is in today's world.
4) Route the headline to the corresponding editor.
5) Editor will select only those headlines for print which has a relevancy score of 4 or more.
	This selection is automatic, but editor has an option of disabling this or selecting any headline that has a score < 4

As an extension to this use case, the following points can also be considered:

6) Reflection / Quality Check	
Re-check whether the headline classification and score are justified. If confidence is low, send for human review.

7) Article Generation
For score ≥ 4, generate a short article draft from the headline.

8) Fact-Checking Agent
Verify whether the headline/article contains unsupported claims, exaggeration, or sensitive content.

9) Editorial Style Agent
Rewrite the article based on newspaper style: neutral tone, crisp headline, summary, and body.

10) Risk / Compliance
Check	Check for defamation, hate speech, political sensitivity, misinformation, or legal risk.

11) Priority Ranking
Rank all printable headlines by importance, urgency, public interest, and category balance.

12) Page Placement Decision	
Decide whether the article goes to front page, category page, digital-only, or “hold for later.”

13) Memory Update
Store past editor decisions, rejected headlines, preferred writing style, and category-wise scoring patterns.

14	Feedback Loop
After editor approval/rejection, update future scoring rules and routing logic.

15	Publishing Action
Send approved articles to CMS, email editor, create CSV/JSON output, or generate final newspaper queue.
